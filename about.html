As a former lawyer turned aspiring software engineer, Matt Lumpkin brings a unique blend of analytical precision and creative problem-solving to the tech world. Currently enrolled in MIT’s Full Stack Development Certificate Program, Matt is committed to mastering software engineering. Matt’s journey from law to software development is driven by a desire to build solutions. His background in law has honed his critical thinking and attention to detail, skills that seamlessly translate to writing clean, efficient code and developing robust applications. At MIT, Matt is immersing himself in a rigorous curriculum that covers front-end and back-end technologies. He is adept at languages such as JavaScript and Python, and is gaining hands-on experience with frameworks like React and Node.js.
